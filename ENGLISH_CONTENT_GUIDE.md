## How to Update:

1. Go to **Shopify Admin → Online Store → Themes**
2. Click **Customize** on your theme
3. For each section mentioned above:
   - Click on the section
   - Find the text field (heading, description, button label, etc.)
   - Replace Arabic text with English text from this guide
4. Click **Save**

## Note:
You'll need to do this manually for each language, OR use Shopify's **Translate & Adapt** app to manage translations automatically.
